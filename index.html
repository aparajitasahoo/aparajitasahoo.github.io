About Me



I am an innovation engineer at the REDX Innovation Lab, Mumbai in collaboration with the Emerging Worlds Special Interest Group at MIT Media Lab



I am passionate about creating a social impact by working at the intersection of health technologies, data science, design and social innovation that will impact the lives of people globally. I am also interested in enhancing user experiences by combining the power of computing, mobile technologies and effective design. I have also worked on prototyping low cost healthcare devices that can be deployed in rural areas that don't have access to medical facilities.
