Manzil

Landmark based voice driven application for navigation

Local navigation can be challenge in a country like India where people rely on verbal directions and hints given by locals to go from one to another. Very few people use GPS to travel locally, especially in rural areas of India. Many times, the instructions are complicated to remember which makes people forget them and get lost in their way.



Manzil is a landmark based local navigational system. It lets users navigate locally using landmarks that are generated by verbal directions. It consists of a simple interface where the user needs to give verbal instructions to generate the map and use it to navigate.
