Modular eye monitoring device for tracking pupillary movements

Abnormal pupillary movements can be an indication of abnormal health conditions. There is a need to develop a portable device that can be used by a doctor in rural areas to monitor the pupil movements in a patient.

After brainstorming with various ophthalmologists and optometrists at the L V Prasad Eye Institute Hyderabad and Srujana Centre of Innovation, we tried to solve this problem by creating a modular prototype of a portable eye monitoring device. Its design was inspired from the Google Cardboard.
